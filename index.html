Story

I graduated from Ball State University in 2018 with a degree in Hospitality and Theatre. After college, I began working as the Even Manager for the Indianapolis Symphony Orchestra where I grew my event planning skills and customer service skills. Due to the recent pandemic our theatre was forced to close and I wanted to learn a skill that would open up new doors to me. Now, I am learning about web development through Eleven Fifty Academy and hope to work in this career feild after the course.
